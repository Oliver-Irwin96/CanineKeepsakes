# Blank product photos — drop them here

Drop one clean product photo per product into this `blanks/` folder. The mockup generator
**auto-detects** them: if `blanks/<product-slug>.png` (or `.jpg`) exists, it composites the
customer's design onto that real photo; if not, it falls back to the framed placeholder.
No code change needed — drop the file, deploy, done. (I'll fine-tune the exact design
position/size per product once I can see each photo.)

## Naming — exactly the product slug
`blanks/<slug>.png` or `blanks/<slug>.jpg`. Slugs must match the catalog exactly (below).

## What makes a good blank
- Front-facing, plain **blank** product (NO design on it), clean/neutral background.
- Decent resolution (≥ 1000px on the long side).
- The printable surface clearly visible and roughly centred (chest of a tee, wrap of a mug).
- Consistent style across products looks best (all on white, or all lifestyle) — your call.
- Easiest source: your Gelato dashboard → each product → download the plain product/mockup image.
