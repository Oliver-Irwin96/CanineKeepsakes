The deal

The deal

What creators get

  • Creator credit on approved published collections
  • 10% of net profit from eligible collection sales
  • Monthly PayPal payouts once earnings reach £50
  • A shareable collection link
  • Exposure to Canine Keepsakes customers

The rules

  • Original artwork only
  • No copyrighted characters, logos or copied designs
  • CK has final approval on all submissions
  • We may adapt the design to fit our breed and product system
  • Royalties apply only to approved, published and completed sales
  • Refunded, cancelled or removed orders do not earn payouts

Net profit = sale price minus product cost, shipping, payment processing fees and any refunds or chargebacks.

FAQs
Do I keep ownership of my idea?

You remain credited as the creator of the original concept, but by submitting and being approved you allow Canine Keepsakes to adapt, sell and promote the design as part of our store.

Can I submit fan art?

No. Do not submit copyrighted characters, famous brands, logos, celebrities or copied artwork.

How much do creators earn?

Approved creators earn 10% of net profit from eligible sales of their approved collection.

When are payouts made?

Payouts are reviewed monthly and paid by PayPal once your creator balance reaches £50.

Will every submission be accepted?

No. Canine Keepsakes has final say on what gets approved, adapted and published.

Can I promote my collection?

Yes. Approved creators receive a shareable collection link and are encouraged to promote their collection.
