Our story

It started with one very good dog.

Canine Keepsakes started with a simple frustration: most dog gifts feel generic. Same clip-art dog, same safe slogans, same designs that never quite feel like your dog.

We wanted to build something with more character — original artwork collections inspired by the personalities dog owners recognise instantly. The sofa boss. The chaos machine. The suspiciously innocent mud monster. The dog who is absolutely walking you, not the other way around.

So we built collections. Each one starts as an original idea, then gets recreated across our breed library so more owners can find a version that feels personal. Choose the collection, choose the breed, choose the product — or ask us to customise one with your actual dog.

Everything is printed to order, which means less waste, no piles of unwanted stock, and a product made specifically for the person who ordered it.
