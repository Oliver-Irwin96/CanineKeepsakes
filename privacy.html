Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This policy explains what personal data Canine Keepsakes ("we", "us") collects when you use caninekeepsakes.co.uk, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Canine Keepsakes is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. Canine Keepsakes is operated from the United Kingdom. For privacy enquiries, contact us at caninekeepsakes.admin@gmail.com.

2. What we collect

  • Account details — your email address and a securely hashed password, when you create an account.
  • Order & delivery details — your name, email, postal address and the items you order.
  • Payment — payments are handled by PayPal. We do not see or store your card details; PayPal shares with us only confirmation of payment and the amount.
  • Technical data — basic request information (such as IP address) is processed by our hosting provider to deliver and secure the site.
  • Custom artwork submissions — if you use our custom dog artwork service, we collect the photo you upload, your dog's name, breed/mix details, selected design, notes you provide and payment confirmation for the artwork fee.

We use uploaded dog photos only to create and manage your custom artwork request, communicate with you about the preview, and keep a record of the request.

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, and we do not sell your personal data.

4. Who we share it with

We share data only with the service providers we need to run the shop, each acting as our processor or as an independent controller:

  • PayPal — payment processing for product orders and custom artwork fees.
  • Printful — printing and shipping your order (receives your name and delivery address).
  • Supabase — secure database, account/login hosting and private storage for custom artwork uploads (EU region).
  • Netlify — website and serverless hosting.

5. International transfers

Where a provider processes data outside the UK/EEA, that transfer is covered by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or a UK adequacy decision.

6. How long we keep it

We keep account data while your account is active. Order and transaction records are kept for as long as required for tax and accounting purposes (generally six years), then deleted or anonymised.

Custom artwork photos and submission details are kept only for as long as needed to complete the request, handle support, and maintain order records, unless you ask us to delete them sooner where legally possible.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to our processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email caninekeepsakes.admin@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies and local storage

We use only storage that is strictly necessary to make the shop work — we do not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. Specifically:

  • Your basket is stored in your browser's local storage so it persists between pages.
  • When you sign in, a secure session token is stored in your browser so you stay logged in.

Because this storage is essential to the service, it does not require consent, but you can clear it any time via your browser settings.

9. Security

Passwords are hashed by our authentication provider, payment card data never touches our servers, and access to order data is restricted so each customer can only see their own orders.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top shows the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email caninekeepsakes.admin@gmail.com.
