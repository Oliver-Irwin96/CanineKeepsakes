FAQs
Is this a full custom drawing?

No. We customise one of our existing designs by replacing the dog with your dog.

Can you change the background?

No. Backgrounds, scenes and collection themes stay the same.

Can you do mixed-breed dogs?

Yes. Upload a clear photo and tell us the breed mix if you know it.

Do I get a preview?

Yes. We send a digital preview before anything is printed.

What if the likeness is slightly off?

One revision is included for colour, markings or likeness tweaks.

Can I order the artwork on different products?

Yes. Once approved, the artwork can be used on available Canine Keepsakes products.
