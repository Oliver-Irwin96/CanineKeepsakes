Accessibility

Last updated: 9 July 2026

We want everyone to enjoy Canine Keepsakes, however they browse. We aim to meet the internationally recognised WCAG 2.2 Level AA standard, building the site with keyboard navigation, clear focus states, scalable readable text, sensible colour contrast, descriptive alternative text, properly labelled forms and a logical heading structure.

We test our site on Chrome, Edge, Firefox and Safari, on mobile, and using keyboard-only navigation. As a small, growing shop we're always improving and there may be corners we haven't perfected yet. If you hit a barrier or need something in a different format, email caninekeepsakes.admin@gmail.com with the page and the problem — we'll help you directly and prioritise a fix.
